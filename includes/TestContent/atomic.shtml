Atomic Principles

Principles around developing a component-based system for fast development and prototyping

On small screens (phones and small tablets), there is often very little room for traditional layout patterns such as two-, three- or four-columns floated beside each other. Instead, the content is usually laid out in a list style - sections or elements stacked on top of one another requiring the user to scroll vertically to gain access to the content. Coupled with this are often horizontally or vertically sliding menu panels.
